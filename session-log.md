## 2026-06-22  Phase 2  Build Layer 2 Elo ratings
- Did: Implemented ratings.py as a transparent, hand-written Elo, separated from I/O. expected_score is the standard logistic expectation with the home advantage added to the home side, returning a value in the open unit interval. compute_elo processes matches chronologically by date, initialises unseen teams at base_rating, scores 1, 0.5, 0 for win, draw, loss, and moves both ratings by k_factor times actual minus expected, applying the home advantage to the home team only at non-neutral venues. The update is zero-sum and the result is sorted highest to lowest. Set as_of_date to 2026-06-10, the pre-tournament baseline, and added configurable elo params (base_rating 1500, k_factor 32, home_advantage 65) to config.yaml. Wired pipeline.py to compute ratings, record a ratings summary in the run log, and print the top 20. Placeholder predictions stay in place. Goal-difference and tournament-importance weighting were deliberately left out as later ablation factors.

## 2026-06-22  Phase 4  Build Layer 4 squad-value adjustment
- Did: Implemented squad.py with CSV reading separated from pure logic. load_squad_values reads a dated, committed snapshot, validates the team and market_value_eur_m columns, and returns a team to value dict, raising a clear error on a missing file or column. adjust_ratings is pure: it converts each team's squad value to a z-score across the rated field, nudges the Elo rating by weight times that z-score, and clamps the nudge to plus or minus max_adjustment so Elo stays the backbone. A team at the field mean is unchanged and a team with no value is left as is.
